Care Tips for First-Time Owners

Nutrition

Choose life-stage food; introduce gradually over 5–7 days.

Litter & Hygiene

1 litter box per cat + 1 extra; scoop daily to reduce odor.

Enrichment

Daily play sessions; rotate toys to keep novelty high.

Health & Safety

Schedule annual vet checkups; keep toxic plants out of reach.

Grooming

Brush weekly (more for long hair); introduce nail trims slowly.

Travel & Stress

Use carriers with soft bedding; practice short rides first.
