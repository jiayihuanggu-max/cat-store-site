About Xue.

We are a warm, cat-friendly shop dedicated to giving stray cats a safe, loving home. We focus on high-quality essentials and care that improve every cat’s daily life. Our goal is simple: happy, confident cats.

Our Values

  • Welfare first — comfort, safety, and enrichment.
  • Clear guidance — demystify first-time cat care.
  • Community — adoption partners.
